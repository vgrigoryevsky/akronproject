# Earth Applications

A structured inventory of applications dedicated solely to addressing problems and needs on Earth, using sensing, data, and analytics to support environmental management, infrastructure, industry, and society. This table includes only terrestrial use cases and explicitly excludes space exploration, space environment characterisation, or any applications whose primary purpose is beyond Earth.

## EAPP-1: Medical Diagnostic Imaging

Advanced X-ray and related imaging systems for diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment planning in healthcare.

## EAPP-2: Radiation Safety and Nuclear Facility Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of radiation levels in nuclear facilities and surrounding environments for safety and compliance.

## EAPP-3: Precision Industrial Metrology

High-stability measurement systems ensuring calibration traceability and quality control in advanced manufacturing.

## EAPP-4: Critical Infrastructure Timing and Synchronisation

High-precision timing for telecommunications, energy grids, financial transactions, and distributed industrial systems.

## EAPP-5: Autonomous Industrial Operations

AI-enabled adaptive sensing and control in manufacturing, energy production, and logistics environments.

## EAPP-6: Environmental Hazard Monitoring

Detection of volcanic activity, landslides, permafrost thaw, and other geophysical hazards affecting communities.

## EAPP-7: Urban Environmental Monitoring

Dense sensor networks monitoring air quality, heat islands, and environmental stressors in cities.

## EAPP-8: Open Science and Long-Term Data Stewardship

Preservation and interoperability of scientific datasets to enable reproducibility, transparency, and cross-disciplinary reuse.

## EAPP-9: Industrial Edge Analytics and IoT Sensing

Local processing and intelligent analysis of high-volume sensor data within industrial and infrastructure systems.

## EAPP-10: Geodetic Surveying and Earth Deformation Monitoring

Precision gravity, magnetic, and positioning measurements for tectonics, land subsidence, and Earth system science.

## EAPP-11: Public Health UV Exposure Monitoring

Monitoring ultraviolet radiation exposure levels to inform public health advisories and prevention strategies.

## EAPP-12: Energy Sector Subsurface Exploration

Electromagnetic and gravity-based sensing to characterise subsurface structures for geothermal and other energy resources.

## EAPP-13: Digital Preservation and Reproducible Analytics

Long-term preservation of raw data and computational workflows enabling reliable reprocessing and regulatory traceability.

## EAPP-14: Air Quality and Emissions Monitoring

Detection and quantification of atmospheric pollutants and greenhouse gases to support regulation, compliance, and public health protection.

## EAPP-15: Climate Monitoring and Benchmarking

High-accuracy long-term monitoring of radiation balance, temperature trends, and atmospheric composition for climate attribution and policy support.

## EAPP-16: Disaster Risk Reduction and Emergency Response

Rapid sensing and analytics to support response to floods, wildfires, storms, volcanic events, and industrial accidents.

## EAPP-17: Industrial Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

High-resolution inspection of materials and structures using X-ray and electromagnetic techniques without causing damage.

## EAPP-18: Infrastructure Subsurface Mapping

Imaging underground utilities, foundations, and geological structures to reduce construction risk and asset failure.

## EAPP-19: Water Resource and Groundwater Management

Mapping and monitoring subsurface water resources and hydrological change to support sustainable water planning.

## EAPP-20: Weather Forecasting and Atmospheric Services

Operational wind, aerosol, and trace gas measurements supporting numerical weather prediction and severe weather warnings.
